Our Leadership

Waleed Arif is the Founder and Chief Executive Officer of A&T Nexus, driving the company’s mission to enable digital transformation for small and medium-sized enterprises. With a background in Petroleum Engineering and experience across international and remote corporate environments, he combines technical expertise with strategic leadership to deliver scalable, results-driven solutions.

Rooted in the business and industrial sector, Waleed’s early exposure to entrepreneurship shaped his perspective on leadership, resilience, and sustainable growth. His leadership journey includes serving as President of the SPE UCSI Student Chapter, where he guided the chapter to win the Presidential Award for Outstanding Student Chapter from SPE International, USA—placing it among the top 5% globally. He also received the Outstanding Student Leader Award from SPE Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, recognizing his distinguished achievements in leadership and community impact.

At A&T Nexus, Waleed emphasizes operational excellence, innovation, and integrity,
positioning the company as a trusted partner for SMEs seeking affordable and impactful
digital solutions.
